About

Alexander is a storyboard artist from Cheshire based in Bristol. He enjoys telling character focused stories with an emphasis on caricature. Alongside storyboarding he is a passionate drawer always armed with a variety of sketchbooks and pens ready to capture the moment as a continuous line drawing or an expressive caricature.
